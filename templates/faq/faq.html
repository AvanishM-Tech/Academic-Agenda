Frequently Asked Questions

What is Academic Agenda and what does it do?

Academic Agenda is a website that helps students collaborate and chat with each other. It also allows students to create groups for specific topics or projects.

How do I create an account on Academic Agenda?

To create an account on Academic Agenda, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the instructions.

How do I create a group on Academic Agenda?

To create a group on Academic Agenda, log in to your account and click on the "Create Group" button. Follow the instructions to set up your group.
